Garlic Naan

Description

An extremely popular indian flatbread recipe made with plain flour and garlic. Naan pairs well with North Indian or Punjabi curries and can be served for lunch and dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp garlic

For Garlic Butter

  • 3 tbsp butter (melted)
  • 1 tsp garlic (grated)
  • 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl mix 2 cup flour, 1/4 tsp baking soda and 1 tsp baking powder and 3/4 tsp salt.
  2. Mix in 2 tbsp oil, 1.4 cup yogurt and 1 tsp garlic.
  3. Add water as required to knead into a smooth, soft dough.
  4. Grease the dough with oil and cover to rest for 2 hours.
  5. Prepare garlic butter by mixing 3 tbsp butter, 1 tsp garlic, 2 tbsp coriander
  6. After 2 hours, knead dough to remove air and pinch ball sized dough shapes
  7. Roll balls to a flat show with rolling pin.
  8. Uniformly coat naan surface with water.
  9. Place watered side of naan on a heated non-stick pan and slightly press.
  10. Flip pan upside down and cook naan directly on flame until golden brown.
  11. Brush naan with garlic butter and remove from pan.
