Mango Lassi

Description

An Indian yogurt drink made with mangoes. Smooth, creamy and delicious, it is an excellent way to cool your tongue after eating a spicy curry!

Ingredients

  • 2 mangos - peeled, seeded and diced
  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine mangos, yogurt, sugar and ice.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve.
